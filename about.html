A bit more about me!

Why I like to take pictures?
It is because I was able to cherish more moments when I started to take pictures.
Fortunately, I had many chances to visit many different areas since I was young.
I could experience a lot many different food and places which enabled me to be open minded and value diversity.
Through taking pictures, I can keep the moments and recall them whenver I want.

Photojournalism

As I mentioned in the home section, I learned photojournalism in Singapore. As I worked as a journalist in Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea when I was studying there, the photo skills and the responsibility as a photojournalist helped me a lot as I came back to Korea.


The Sungkyun Times

From 2015-2017, at Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea, I was a journalist who writes article for the university. I was lucky to interview many students and professors from the university and also many insightful people in the society. I also took the photos for my articles by myself. If you are interested, visit the website! The Sungkyun Times Website
